About me

I am a PhD student at Princeton University in the programming languages group, currently working on the CertiCoq project. I obtained my Master’s degree from ENS Cachan (Master Parisien de Recherche en Informatique) and my diploma (5-year engineering degree) from National Technical University of Athens.

I was a research intern at Max Planck Institute of Software Systems (March - August 2015) and INRIA (April - September 2014). I am very happy to have been advised by Deepak Garg (MPI-SWS) and Cătălin Hriţcu (INRIA).

Research Interests

My general interests lie in the area of programming languages theory and implementation, program analysis, formal verification and their applications to the reliability and security of software.
